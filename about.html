About TechConf 2026

Our Story

TechConf Romania started in 2020 as a small meetup of 50 tech enthusiasts in Timișoara. Over the years, it has grown into one of Eastern Europe's most anticipated technology conferences, attracting over 500 attendees annually.

Our mission is to create a platform where developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and tech leaders can share knowledge, build connections, and shape the future of technology together. We believe in accessible, inclusive, and practical tech education.

TechConf 2026 marks our 7th edition, featuring 3 days of inspiring talks, hands-on workshops, and unparalleled networking opportunities.

Meet Our Speakers

Dr. Maria Popescu

Maria is a Research Lead at DeepMind, focusing on large language models and their applications in healthcare. With a PhD in Machine Learning from University of Cambridge, she has published over 30 papers in top AI conferences. Her keynote will explore the current state and future possibilities of generative AI technologies.

Keynote: "The Future of Generative AI: Beyond ChatGPT"

Session Time: June 15, 09:30 - Main Hall

Andrei Ionescu

Andrei is a Senior Developer Advocate at GitHub, where he helps developers navigate the open-source ecosystem. He's the maintainer of several popular open-source projects with over 10,000 GitHub stars combined. His talk will cover best practices for building and sustaining successful open-source projects.

Talk: "Building in Public: Lessons from Open Source Success Stories"

Session Time: June 15, 11:00 - Workshop Room A

Elena Dumitrescu

Elena is the CTO and co-founder of a rapidly growing Romanian tech startup that was recently acquired. She has over 15 years of experience in software architecture and team leadership. Her session will share practical insights on scaling engineering teams and technology stacks.

Talk: "From Zero to Scale: Technical and Cultural Lessons"

Session Time: June 16, 09:30 - Main Hall

Vlad Georgescu

Vlad leads the Cloud Infrastructure team at a major European tech company. He specializes in Kubernetes, serverless architectures, and cloud cost optimization. His workshop will be a hands-on session about deploying scalable applications on modern cloud platforms.

Workshop: "Cloud-Native Applications: From Zero to Production"

Session Time: June 16, 14:00 - Lab Room B
